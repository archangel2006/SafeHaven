Medical Help
For life-threatening emergencies, call emergency services immediately!

First-Aid Instructions

For Bleeding:

  • Stay calm and assess the wound severity
  • Apply direct pressure with a clean cloth or bandage
  • Elevate the wound above heart level if possible
  • Do not remove embedded objects
  • Seek immediate medical attention for severe bleeding

For Burns:

  • Cool the burned area with running water for 10-20 minutes
  • Do not apply ice directly to burns
  • Cover with a clean, dry cloth or sterile bandage
  • Do not break blisters
  • Seek medical help for serious burns

For Fractures:

  • Immobilize the injured area
  • Do not try to realign the bone
  • Apply ice pack wrapped in cloth to reduce swelling
  • Avoid moving the injured person unless necessary
  • Call for professional medical assistance

General Emergency Actions:

  • Check for responsiveness and breathing
  • Call emergency services if conditions worsen
  • Keep the person warm and comfortable
  • Monitor vital signs while waiting for help
  • Do not give food or drink if surgery may be needed

Voice Control Instructions

  • Click the "Start Voice Control" button to begin
  • Speak clearly into your microphone
  • Try commands like: "Hello", "Help", "Evacuate", "Call for help", "Medical emergency"
  • The system will process your command and respond accordingly
  • Use "Stop" to end voice control

Available Commands:

  • "Hello" - Greet the assistant
  • "Help" or "Call for help" - Request emergency assistance
  • "Evacuate" - Get evacuation instructions
  • "Medical emergency" - Dispatch medical help
  • "Fire" - Report fire emergency
  • "Status" - Check system status
  • "Stop" - End voice control

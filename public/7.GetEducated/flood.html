What is a Flood?

Floods occur when water overflows onto normally dry land. This can happen due to heavy rainfall, storm surges, or melting snow.

Safety Measures During a Flood

  • Move to higher ground and avoid areas near rivers or streams.
  • Do not drive through flooded areas.
  • Ensure you have an emergency kit with food, water, and first aid supplies.

Real-Life Flood Cases

The 2010 Pakistan floods affected millions, displacing entire communities and causing widespread damage.

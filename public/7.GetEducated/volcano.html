Safety Measures During a Volcanic Eruption

  • Evacuate Immediately: Follow authorities' instructions if you are in a danger zone.
  • Protect Against Ash: Ash is pulverized rock, not wood ash. Wear N95 masks and goggles.
  • Shelter in Place: If caught indoors, close all windows, doors, and fireplace dampers to keep ash out.
  • Avoid Low Ground: Stay away from valleys where lahars (mudflows) and lava travel.

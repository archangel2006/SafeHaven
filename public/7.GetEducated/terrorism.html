What is a Chemical or Terrorist Attack?

Chemical or biological attacks use harmful substances to cause destruction and terror. They may involve chemical agents, biological agents, or radiological substances.

Safety Measures During a Chemical Attack

  • Stay indoors and seal windows and doors to avoid exposure to toxins.
  • If outside, cover your nose and mouth with a cloth or mask.
  • Stay informed by following official channels for updates and evacuation instructions.

Real-Life Terrorism/Chemical Attack Cases

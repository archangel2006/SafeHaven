Earthquake Safety

What is an Earthquake?

Earthquakes are sudden shaking movements of the Earth's surface caused by the movement of tectonic plates. They can cause massive destruction and loss of life.

Safety Measures During an Earthquake

  • Drop to your knees, cover your head, and hold onto something sturdy.
  • If you are outside, move to an open area away from buildings, trees, and power lines.
  • Stay inside if you are indoors; avoid doorways and windows.

Real-Life Earthquake Cases

In 2011, the Great East Japan Earthquake caused widespread devastation, with a magnitude of 9.1.

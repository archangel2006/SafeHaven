Wildfire Safety

What is a Wildfire?

Wildfires are uncontrolled fires that spread rapidly through vegetation, often driven by wind. They can occur in forests, grasslands, or any place with dry vegetation.

Safety Measures During a Wildfire

  • Evacuate early if you are in an area at risk.
  • Stay indoors if there is smoke, and keep windows and doors closed.
  • Have a kit with masks, water, and essential items to survive a wildfire.
